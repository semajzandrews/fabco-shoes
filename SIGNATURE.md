# Fabco Shoes — SIGNATURE

## Concept: "THE FLOOR"
Fabco is an affordable family shoe store (chain since 1953) on Main St in the City
of Orange, NJ. Instead of a discount-bin look, present the store like a calm,
modern retail FLOOR rendered as a bento mosaic of product tiles you step across:
sneakers, boots, dress, kids, sandals, accessories. Clean-tech grid energy applied
to a neighborhood shoe store reads premium and fair, never cheap.

## Signature moment: THE SIZE RUNNER
An interactive bento "fit bar" in the hero region. A horizontal size scale
(youth → adult, e.g. 1 / 4 / 7 / 9 / 11 / 13) sits inside a wide tile. As the
visitor drags or hovers a slider thumb across it, a real shoe in the adjacent tile
swaps and the copy updates ("We carry the whole family, from a kid's size 1 to a
men's 13"). It encodes the ONE true pitch of a family shoe store: every size, every
member of the household, one floor. Pointer-driven, idle auto-sweep so it lives
untouched, keyboard accessible, degrades to a static labeled scale under
reduced-motion. Distinct from past signatures (no dial, no shader, no line-draw,
no breathing ring): it is a SIZE/FIT instrument tied to the bento grid.
